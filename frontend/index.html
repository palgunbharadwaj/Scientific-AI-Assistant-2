About Our Architecture

This platform utilizes specialized multi-agent AI modules to assist in high-level scientific reasoning securely and efficiently.

Chemistry Research Agent (CRA)
Analyzes chemical properties, molecular stability, and how substances react with each other in a lab setting.
Drug Discovery Agent (DDRA)
Identifies biological targets for new drugs, predicts toxicity, and helps design effective medical treatments.
Prescription Evaluation Agent (DPEA)
Checks drug safety, calculates correct dosages, and identifies potential clinical side effects.
Pathology & Disease Research Agent (PDRA)
Analyzes disease mechanisms, tissue structural changes, and blood samples for diagnostic research.
